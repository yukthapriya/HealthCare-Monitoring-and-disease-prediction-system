SNACK

MONDAY

2 tsp fenugreek seeds(soaked overnight), 2 millet  vegetable cheela/dosa, and a cup of tea or coffee or low-fat milk.

1 cup mixed vegetable salad , 2 multigrain chapati OR 1 chapati and ½ bowl brown rice 1 and bowl dal or chicken .

1 cup lemon tea without sugar/honey and ¼ cup roasted chana.

1 chapati and 2 bowls ofvegetables and salad.

½ cup milk without sugar.

TUESDAY

2 tsp fenugreek seeds(soaked overnight), vegetable oats, and a cup of tea or coffee or low-fat milk.

2 chapatis (50% ragi) with vegetable salad and low-fat paneer/curd.

1 cup lemon tea without sugar/honey and ¼ cup roasted chana.

1 chapati and mixed vegetables and salad with 1 bowl of fish/dal.

½ cup milk without sugar.

WEDNESDAY

2 tsp fenugreek seeds(soaked overnight), 2-egg whites with 1 whole wheat toast with grilled vegetable  and a cup of tea or coffee or low-fat milk.

2 chapatis (50% jowar) with vegetable salad and low-fat paneer/chicken (50gm).

1 cup lemon tea without sugar/honey and ¼ cup roasted chana.

1 chapati (50% jowar) and vegetables and salad with 1 bowl of fish/dal.

½ cup milk without sugar.

THURSDAY

2 tsp fenugreek seeds(soaked overnight, milk with oats or dalia and a cup of tea or coffee or low-fat milk.

2 chapatis (50% ragi) with vegetable salad and low-fat paneer/curd.

1 cup lemon tea without sugar/honey and ¼ cup roasted chana.

1 beasan cheela with vegetables and salad.

½ cup milk without sugar.

FRIDAY

2 tsp fenugreek seeds(soaked overnight), idli/poha/upma  and a cup of tea or coffee or low-fat milk.

2 chapatis (50% chana) with  mixed vegetables,  salad and low-fat paneer/curd.

1 cup lemon tea without sugar/honey and ¼ cup roasted chana.

1 chapati (50% jowar) and vegetables and salad with 1 bowl of fish/dal.

½ cup milk without sugar.

SATURDAY

2 tsp fenugreek seeds(soaked overnight), whole wheat sandwich / chicken sandwich and a cup of tea or coffee or low-fat milk.

2 chapatis (knead with any green leafy vegetable ), vegetable salad and 1 bowl of sprout or 1 egg white curry.

1 cup lemon tea without sugar/honey and ¼ cup roasted chana.

1 chapati (50% jowar) and vegetables and salad with 1 bowl of fish/dal.

½ cup milk without sugar.

SUNDAY

2 tsp fenugreek seeds(soaked overnight, moong dal  cheela with vegetables or stuffed multi grain chapati , and a cup of tea or coffee or low-fat milk.

1 cup of brown rice, vegetable salad and 1 bowl of chana/ chicken.

1 cup lemon tea without sugar/honey and ¼ cup roasted chana.

1 chapati and 2 bowls of vegetables and salad.

½ cup milk without sugar.
